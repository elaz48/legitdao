Founders

In the LegitDAO ecosystem, the founders are the individuals who originally conceived the project and provided the necessary resources to launch it. They were responsible to plan the initial development of the technology, the tokenomics, and initiate the promotion of the venture in order to form the initial LegitDAO community.

There are six founding members in LegitDAO, and each will hold 16,666,666 tokens within this smart contract. This results in a total cap of 99,999,996 tokens for the contract. Additionally, the contract will feature a marketplace where founders can sell their tokens to the community in exchange for BNB coins.

There is no tax to trade the founder’s units.

Affiliate

The affiliate smart-contract gives the ability to refer people to our ecosystem. The smart-contract gives the ability to people to register wallets under their own wallet. When someone referred someone else, that person is also added as the second’s person affiliate which build a multi-level affiliate tree.

There is also a marketplace in the affiliate smart-contract, allowing people to buy and sell affiliate tree in exchange of BNB coins. There is no fees in the marketplace in order to trade affiliate trees.

Initially, people referred their friends and family into our ecosystem during its planning phase. We build a community of around 300 members during that phase. Initially, the contract will contain the affiliate tree of these people that were manually referred.

DAO

The DAO smart-contract is an ERC-20 smart-contract with additional abilities. The DAO smart-contract contain tokens that will be assigned to the members of our community. These members consists of friends and family of the founders. The smart-contract contains tokens that will be initially assigned to its community members pro-rate to their contribution. Each member paid an amount of their choice as their contribution to be part of the DAO. 15% of their contribution will be assigned to the referral tree that referred them. We accumulated a total of 1.6M$CAD in contributions, which were kept safely in bitcoin during the planification phase of the project. At launch of this smart-contract, the funds will be transfered to BNB coins and sent to the DAO smart-contract to be managed by its members.

Real-world example

So, let’s say the project accumulated 1.6M$ in total, and the DAO contains 115M tokens. Jack gave a contribution of 2000$ and referred Anna and contributed 1$ and then Anna referred Johnny that contributed 1000$ to the project.

  1. Jack would receive 125k tokens of the DAO because of its contributions (2000/1600000) * 100000000
  2. Anna would receive 62 tokens of the DAO because of its contributions (1/1600000) * 100000000
  3. Johnny would receive 62,5k tokens of the DAO because of its contributions (1000/1600000) * 100000000
  4. Anna would receive 9375 tokens because she referred Johnny (62500 * 0,15)
  5. Jack would receive 9 tokens because he referred Anna (621406 * 0,15) and 1406 tokens because he referred Anna that referred Johnny (9375 * 0,15)

Therefore …

  1. Jack would have 126,415 tokens
  2. Anna would have 9437 tokens
  3. Johnny would receive 62,000 tokens

Token minting

The DAO contract will mint 100% of its unit at deployment of the contract. No other tokens will ever be minted in the future.

Marketplace in the DAO smart-contract

In the DAO smart contract, there is a marketplace where people can buy and sell tokens using BNB coins.

Transfer Tax

When transfering DAO tokens, which includes selling them on the marketplace or transfering them to any other wallet, there is a tax of 20% when initiating the transfer, and 15% when receiving a transfer. Those fees are paid to the people that referred the buyer and seller. If they were not referred by anyone, the fees goes to the founging member contract.

Service-provider smart contract

The service-developer smart contract is a smart contract that is developed by service-providers that wants to create propositions to the DAO. Each service provider will need to take the solidity code of that smart contract, deploy it and connect their deployed smart contract to the proposition smart contract in order to be eligible to execute contracts for the DAO.

The service-provider smart contract is an ERC-20 smart contract, that have the ability to manage developers and tasks openly.

This system enables openness and gives the ability for the DAO to see what tasks have been executed by the developer on their open contract with them. It also gives the ability to the DAO token holders to see which developer have been paid, for which task at what time.

Transfer Tax

The service-provider contains a transfer tax of 0,25% for the person that receives the tokens, and 0.25% for the person that send the tokens. Those fees are paid to the affiliate tree that referred the service provider. If the service provider was not referred by anyone, the fees are sent to the founder smart-contract.

Developer smart contract

The developer smart contract consists of the first service-provider in the LegitDAO ecosystem. That developer created all the initial research and development of the project and created a lot of code that will be onboarded in the LegitDAO ecosystem. It uses the service-provider smart-contract code.

Currency

The currency is an ERC-20 smart-contract that contains a total of 100M. 200k of those units will be assigned at launch to the developer’s smart contract. The reaining will be sold for BNB at launch. The price will begin at 0.00001 BNB.

Every time a token is sold, the price of the next token will double in price.

80% BNB accumulated by the currency smart contract will be sent to the developer’s smart contract, 5% will be sent to the DAO and 15% will be sent as referral payment. If the buyer was referred by someone it will be sent to the referral tree. However, if that person was not referred by anyone, it will be sent to the founder’s smart-contract.

Transfer Tax

Every time a transfer occurs, 0.25% of the transfer will be paid by the sender of tokens, and 0,25% of the transfer will be paid by the receiver. Those fees are paid to the developer smart-contract.

Marketplace

The currency smart-contract also contains a marketplace giving it the ability to buy and sell tokens for BNB coins.

Proposition

The proposition smart contract contains no currency. It enables the ability for service providers to register as a potential proposition writer. The DAO approves or denies service-providers depending on the quality of the public information of the service-provider.

Then, an accepted service-provider can send a proposal to this contract. The DAO then votes to accept or deny the proposition depending on the importance of the proposition to reach the goal of the DAO, which is to build the necessary tools to build an artificial general intelligence (AGI).

When a proposition is accepted, every service-providers can apply to execute the proposition by giving a price in our currency to execute the project, and a deadline to accomplish the mision.

Then, the DAO votes to accept a service-provider to execute a proposition. At this point, the currency is locked in the DAO smart-contract.

Upon a proposal finished by the service provider, the DAO votes again to verify the work quality of the service-provider. If successful, the service-provider is paid automatically by the DAO smart-contract and the proposal is flagged as successful. Otherwise the proposal is rejected and flagged as unsuccessful and the locked currency is unlocked on the DAO smart contract.
